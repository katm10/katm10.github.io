Our
Purpose

We at AccelARate understand and empathize with teachers who are underfunded and are unable to pay for expensive resources to assist their lectures. We hope to solve this problem without emptying pockets.

#AccelarateUnderstands

AccelARate was made by students, for students. We at AccelARate have struggled through projectile motion just as you have, and we're here to make it easier for the next class.
