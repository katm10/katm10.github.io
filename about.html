About

Katherine is a ninth grader at Burlingame High School. There, she is a member of the Interact Club, Girls Who Code Club, and Beyond Borders Club, as well as a dedicated worker in the business section of BHS's robotics team, the Iron Panthers. In her free time, Katherine enjoys playing tennis, as she is on the BHS tennis team, and finding new opportunities in her area, such as volunteering and articipating in coding classes, challenges, and hackathons. In fact, Katherine recently got second in the Resilience Challenge, a social impact challenge in San Francisco. Recently, she also attended the Girls Who Code Udacity Hack-a-Thon, the She++ 3rd Annual Gala, and the GTGTC Code Camp at Stanford. Katherine is very passionate about STEM, and more specifically, computer science, and loves learning more about science with others who share her passion.
