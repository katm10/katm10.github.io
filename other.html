Iron Panthers

2015-Present

At Burlingame High School, I am a member of our robotics team, the Iron Panthers. On this team, I help manage the business side of things by organizing fundraising and outreach events, appling our team for awards and grant, and reaching out to big companies for sponsorships and donations. It has been so much fun being able to help my team grow and excel in our competitions, and next year, I will be the Iron Panthers' fundraising lead and treasurer. This teaches me about leadership, management, bookkeeping, and fundraising. 

Iron Panthers is an award-winning FIRST Robotics team, and it enables young people to become involved in STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) fields. If you would like to donate to the Iron Panthers, simply contact me with the icons below or email cwade@smuhsd.org. 

BHS Girls' Tennis

2015-Present

In the summer of 2015, I began playing tennis, and I fell in love with the sport. Thus, I decided to join the BHS girls' tennis team. On this team, I got to meet new people and cntinue learning about my favorite sport. I even got to play with the juniors regularly, even though I am only a freshman! I plan to stay on this team throughout my years at BHS, and you can usually find me playing tennis with my friends on Wednesday afternoons, even when the season is over.
